The chart, in four moves

How a booking actually happens.

STEP 01

Choose your care

Cleaning, whitening, a filling, or "I'm not sure yet" — the widget above prices it for you instantly.

STEP 02

Pick a real slot

You're only shown chairs that are genuinely open that day — nothing gets double-booked behind the scenes.

STEP 03

Leave your details

A phone number and name is all we need to hold the chair. Notes are optional but help the dentist prep.

STEP 04

Get your code

A confirmation code appears immediately, with an option to add the visit straight to your calendar.
